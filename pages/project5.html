Logo Reveal Animation

Designed and produced a captivating logo reveal animation for LSR Constructions, an esteemed construction company, enhancing their brand presence with dynamic visual engagement.



Tools : Adobe After effects



This is a construction company which works on real estate and construction work. So I decided to design a logo which reflects buildings rising from the ground. So designed round edged rectangular box as background with diagonal lines pattern from left bottom towards right top. I used two different shades of blue to bring 3D depth for the pattern as it looks like a building. On top of that shape typed LSR company name and made it simple and emphasized with bold white colour font.

Animation

My logo reveals animation should replicate formation of blocks, patterns and diagonal lines etc. so that It connects viewers with the meaning of ‘construction’. At the same time it should be royal and rich in design and shape. For this animation, I use principles like ease ,timing , dimensionality and space etc. first pattern appears full frame with diagonal lines, then rotates clockwise and then lines make another pattern in opposite direction. Next, the rectangular shapes appear so the remaining part is masked. Finally, company name forms as masking and unmasking parelley. In the last frame, name appears as static and ends.


Call for action: The Brand Promotion


Styleframes

After trying different styles, different colour effects, I decided to go with this final logo because its simple and emphasizing logo and company name effectively. And also color shades brings architectural 3D feeling as background. I decided this colour to represent energetic characterstic of the company.
