Designing an iOS App For A Furniture Company.

I had to design an iOS app for Humber college industrial students venture which sells furniture products as part of my academic project.



Tools : Figma,Miro,Illustrator


I am in the process of creating an iOS application that aims to transform how industrial students at Humber College present and market their handcrafted products. The app is called

HumberCraft

Target Audience


I believe my intended audience consists of students and middle-class families all over Canada who have an appreciation for stylish furniture but may not have the budget for high-end brands. In this context, students are open to offering these items at a reduced or discounted price in exchange for valuable user feedback.

Demographics

  • Diverse demographic students
  • Ages in between 17-40
  • Mostly tech-savvy people

Apps used by Target Audience

Usability Testing


I gathered feedback during testing, indicating that the "50% off" font size was perceived as too large. Additionally, the navigation icons, including the hamburger menu, seemed oversized. In response, I replaced the hamburger menu with a cart icon, considering its relevance.


The feedback also pointed out a mismatch between the accent color and the gold color in navigation. To address this, I recommend using a different typeface for the categories banner and incorporating a more accessible background color. Consequently, I adjusted the typeface and the navigation bar background color to blue for improved aesthetics.


And I received feedback that payment checkout page font size is too small and separate different sections with more gap.I changed font size and increased the gap. Lastly, Previous logo was not aesthetically good. So I changed it to simple square shaped with alphabet in it.
